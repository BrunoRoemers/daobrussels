What's going on this month? Glad you asked...

**Two lighting talks** narrowing in on payments via blockchain. It sounds like something that should have been figured out by now. Well, **Friedger** has put **Gnosis Pay** to the test. Let's hear how his experience went.

Furthermore we are welcoming **Kevin** to announce a fresh initiative from camp **Citizen Wallet**. If their plotting and scheming proves successful, it will convert payment processor fees into a seedbed for citizen initiatives. How evil!

By far the biggest fish to fry this evening (hint), is the **commons game** under guidance of **Leen**. Thanks to a contrived setting, you will be able to engage with the real challenges (and opportunities) of democratic decision-making and resource sharing. We believe it is essential to realizing the promise of web3...

**About Crypto Wednesday**
On the first Wednesday of every month, the crypto community in Brussels meets *in real life*. This month is no different!

Crypto Wednesdays are organized bottom-up, with a mix of presentations and guided conversations. Everyone has plenty of opportunity to learn, participate, share and ask questions. It's a great way to meet fellow citizens with similar interests. We have beers and sodas, too.

💡Doors open at **7:30 PM**. Whoever shows up are the right people. Whenever it starts is the right time to start.

**What is DAO Brussels?**
DAO Brussels is a community at the intersection of *crypto* and *Brussels*. On one hand, we want to provide the citizens of Brussels with many opportunities to (safely) learn about web3, and create friendships over a common interest. On the other hand, we want to get our hands dirty and use blockchain technology to make a positive impact on Brussels. You can [read our manifesto here](https://dao.brussels/manifesto). We meet online in Discord, and at least once a month, during our *Crypto Wednesdays*.
