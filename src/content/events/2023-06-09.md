Go from idea to prototype in a weekend and win from the **5K prize pool**!

The first edition of the **ETHBrussels Hackathon** starts on **June 9** (Friday evening) in the premises of coding school Campus 19, on top of the Brussels Central Station.

👉 Make new friends and form teams
👉 Ideate, design, code, or develop a business case
👉 Attend several workshops lead by web3 professionals
👉 Connect and get assistance from our on-site mentor crew

On **June 11** (Sunday afternoon) the best projects will be chosen by the jury and be awarded the following prizes.

🥇 1500 EUR + a one-year subscription to Waltio per team member (worth 1K)
🥈 1000 EUR
🥉 500 EUR

💰 1K bounty from BEPRO Network

This hackathon is open to both coders and non-coders.

Sign up to hack at https://ethbrussels.com and invite your friends!
