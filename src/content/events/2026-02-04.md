Join a cozy evening of chatting and hacking on **decentralized technologies**!

We gather on the **first Wednesday of each month** in the Commons Hub, a beacon of hope and innovation in the center of Brussels. ⭐️

After setting the tone in a brief intro, and grabbing a slice of **pizza**, we'll split in smaller groups ("**pods**") to exchange knowledge, gather feedback and build/explore crypto hands-on.

Topics will be decided on the spot ([open space technology](https://www.liberatingstructures.com/25-open-space-technology/)). Come prepared or show up curious... and bring your **laptop** if you wish.

All fair game: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Nostr, Smart Contracts, DAO governance, ZK proofs...

What has **emerged** before:
\- many discussions\, presentations and workshops
\- generating [yield on BTC](https://dao-brussels-vault.lovable.app/) for the community
\- building and dissecting [Citizen Wallet](https://citizenwallet.xyz/)
\- throwing epic parties such as [Block 26](https://www.blockfest.be/)
\- ideating a decentralized ocean impact network
\- experiments with commitment pooling
\- creating a beer brand with supply\-chain transparency
\- hosting hackathons
\- \.\.\.

If you're a hacker, visionary or blockchain nerd that wants to make a dent in the city, you'll feel right at home!

\-\-\-

P.S. This month the Crypto Wednesday falls in the middle of the Open Source Village. Exceptionally no free coworking during the day, but many relevant events spread over two weeks. Check out the [schedule on their site](https://opensourcevillage.org/).

P.S. Would it be a ridiculous idea to cowork or host events at the Commons Hub? Prices in euros and *on-chain* sweat equity are available on [their site](https://commonshub.brussels/). You'll get warmly introduced on their Friday potluck lunches!

P.S. It's not too late to party with the 100 most active people in the Belgian blockchain scene. Last year was a success... do you really want to miss [Block 26](https://www.blockfest.be/)?
