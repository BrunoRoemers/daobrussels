**What is DAO Brussels?**
DAO Brussels is a community at the intersection of *crypto* and *Brussels*. On one hand, we want to provide the citizens of Brussels with many opportunities to (safely) learn about crypto, and create friendships over a common interest. On the other hand, we want to get our hands dirty and use crypto to make a positive impact on Brussels. We meet online in Discord, and at least once a month, during our *Crypto Wednesdays*.
