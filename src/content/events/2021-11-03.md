Crypto Wednesday every first Wednesday of the month from 7pm till 10pm.

- 7pm-8pm: come'n'join
Come and learn how you can join the crypto (aka Web3) world. There will be different workshop tables to get you started with getting your first crypto with Coinbase/Binance, discovering DeFI with Aave/Compound, minting your first NFTs on OpenSea.io, invest in different projects with Uniswap, 1inch, paraswap, etc. Bring your laptop/smartphone.

- 8pm-9pm: come'n'learn 👩🏼‍🏫
2 talks on crypto + QA

Alice Jones from https://Bloomeria.org
Bloomeria offers NFTs (non-fungible tokens) in order to tokenize plots of land to preserve nature 🌱

MrCryptoRisin will present "CryptoPick", a simple way to develop and test your trading skills while earning crypto and NFTs without risking anything. The short presentations will be followed by an exclusive community battle with NFT prizes to win.

- 9pm-10pm: come'n'chat 💬
Discussion tables on different crypto topics, unconference style
