Crypto Wednesday every first Wednesday of the month from 7pm till 10pm.

We're back at the Citizen Corner in Schaerbeek! Please RSVP so we know how many people are coming and we can implement adequate measures to protect everyone's health.

* 7:00pm: **Welcome**
* 7:10pm: **Nina Caput**
Nina has been building with blockchain technology since 2017. She founded @equineledger and worked for @ArianeeProject. Lately, she is focused on launching a collect-to-earn platform related to racehorses. She will share her experience working in the crypto space and will give us a glimpse of her brand new project, Ypoverse.
* 7:40pm: **Florian Ernotte**
Florian is a lawyer at the bar of Liège who stays up to date with the latest trends and technologies. He founded the website [https://cryptomonnaie.be](https://cryptomonnaie.be) and has addressed many Belgian politicians about cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology at the Belgian Chamber of Representatives.
* 8:10pm: **Discussion Tables**
We'll crowd-source various crypto topics and community knowledge. Grab a chair at one of the many tables, hear and be heard. Example topics: NFTs, DeFi, DAOs, security, blockchain development, legislation, taxes, how to set up a wallet, buy/sell...
If you're new to DAO Brussels and/or want to get involved, feel free to join the welcome table or approach us after the event.
* 9:10pm: **Meet the community**
This Crypto Wednesday we will experiment with a new format designed to highlight what's cooking in the community! The goals are to get people talking, encourage collaboration and give everyone the opportunity to address the crowd. There will still be plenty of time to chat, grab a drink or snack and catch up with the Joneses too :)

Feel free to share this event with all of your friends, foes, degens, normies, crypto-skeptics, representatives and even bitcoin maximalists. Our community is welcome to everyone :)
