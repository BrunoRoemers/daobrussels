🚨 The time and date of the merge, and this watch party, are contingent on reaching the Total Terminal Difficulty of 5.875\*10^18. Check the latest estimate at [https://bordel.wtf/](https://bordel.wtf/). RSVP to the event so DAO Brussels can keep you in the loop as the moment of truth approaches 😉.

We'll grab some snacks, watch the livestream and celebrate (or cry about) the merge when it happens.

Join us and bring your friends for the crypto milestone of the year ✨.

**What is the merge?** One of the coming days, the Ethereum blockchain will transition from Proof of Work, to Proof of Stake. Almost immediately, the Ethereum blockchain will reduce it's energy consumption by more than 99%. This major upgrade will usher in a new era for the ecosystem, and pave the way for major scaling (and other) improvements. Hundreds of devs have been working tirelessly for five years to ensure a flawless merge with zero downtime. All that effort will accumulate soon in a big applause (or a big explosion). Read more about the merge on [https://ethereum.org/en/upgrades/merge/](https://ethereum.org/en/upgrades/merge/) (after you have RSVP'd to this event 😉).
