Crypto Wednesday every first Wednesday of the month from 7pm till 10pm.

- 7pm-8pm: come'n'join
Come and learn how you can join the crypto (aka Web3) world. There will be different workshop tables to get you started with getting your first cryptocurrencies with Coinbase/Binance, discovering DeFI with Aave/Compound, minting your first NFTs on OpenSea.io, invest in different projects with Uniswap, 1inch, paraswap, etc. Bring your laptop/smartphone.

- 8pm-9pm: come'n'learn 👩🏼‍🏫
2 talks on crypto + QA

🎨 Joanie Lemercier, renowned digital artist, will talk about "Ethical NFTs" (see his interview in wired on this topic: https://www.wired.com/story/nfts-hot-effect-earth-climate/)

🔥 Fireside chat with Kris Is, community manager at Gitcoin

- 9pm-10pm: come'n'chat 💬
Discussion tables on different crypto topics, unconference style
