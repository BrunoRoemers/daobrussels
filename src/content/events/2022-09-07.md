Meet the Brussels crypto community on our monthly staple: Crypto Wednesday 🥳

* Are you curious about crypto, blockchain, wallets or bitcoin?
* Are you a crypto expert and like to share?
* Do you have a great idea that you want to buidl with frens?
* Do you want to learn about DAOs by doing?
* Are you young or old? From Belgium or abroad?
* Spreekt ge Vlaams ou français, copain.e?

No matter your answers, you are welcome at the **Citizen Corner in Schaerbeek on July 6, 7pm**. (And every next first Wednesday of the month 😉)

We do things a **little differently**: there will be NO presentations and we’ll use “facilitation techniques” to get you in touch with the right people, no matter your needs.

Expect to have a variety of 1-on-1 conversations, and discussions in small groups. The floor is yours to learn, make friends, gather feedback on your project or lay the foundations to build something new.

Please share this event with all of your friends, foes, degens, regens, normies, crypto-skeptics, representatives and bitcoin maximalists. Our community is welcome to everyone :)
