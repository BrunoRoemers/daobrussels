The DAO Tooling pod investigates what software and coordination mechanisms actually work for small, trust-based groups. We test tools, document our findings, and occasionally build things.

## Current focus

- Evaluating Nostr-based coordination (NIP-72 communities, NIP-99 classifieds)
- Documenting what works and what doesn't for groups of 10–50 people
- Building lightweight tooling that doesn't require blockchain gas fees
