The Commons Library pod maintains a curated and collectively managed catalog of physical and digital resources — books, tools, zines, hardware, and more — that members of DAO Brussels can borrow, reference, or contribute to.

## How it works

Resources are cataloged in a shared document. Members can propose additions, flag items for removal, or request to borrow physical materials at the next meeting.

## Contributing

Bring something to the next meeting, or add it to the catalog document with your name and a short description.
