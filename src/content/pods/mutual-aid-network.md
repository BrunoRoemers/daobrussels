The Mutual Aid Network pod was proposed at the January 2025 meeting. The goal is to build a lightweight, privacy-respecting directory of skills and needs within the DAO Brussels community and its wider network.

## Proposed features

- Skills exchange registry (who can teach what, who wants to learn what)
- Resource sharing map (tools, space, surplus food)
- Connection to existing Brussels mutual aid groups

## Status

This pod is looking for people to take it forward. If you're interested, come to the next meeting.
